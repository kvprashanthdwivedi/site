CRESCENDO: MUSIC HUB OF JIIT

OVERVIEW

A family of avid music enthusiasts who Perform, Promote and Propagate music. An open community that welcomes and motivates fellow students to unleash their Musical talents. The basic philosophy of Crescendo is to provide a platform for everyone who has music in their soul ,across streams and across years from various genres to collaborate musically in live performance sessions and showcase a combination of myriad musical influences, from Hindustani, Carnatic, and Indian folk to contemporary, hip-hop, rock & pop music.

OBJECTIVE

  1. To inculcate the Spirit of Music in each one of us.
  2. Share our Knowledge and Skills
  3. Collaborateand Create
  4. BONUS : Spread Joyand Cheer!

EVENTS ORGANISED BY THE HUB

  1. Unplugged : A musical night full of performances by students from every year.
  2. Euphonium :a musical event especially for the instrumentalists to manifest their talents in the college.
  3. Voice Of Impressions : an event for vocalists to show their skills and claim the spot of ‘voice of impressions’.

JPEG : THE PHOTOGRAPHY HUB

OVERVIEW

Aim of this hub is to provide basic learning in the field of photography by introducing to various photography tools, gadgets (like DSLR, tripods, various lenses etc.) and related skills. Often the hub conducts workshops to brush up editing skills and provide basic learning to new comers by introducing various techniques. The hub also conducts photo-walks in and around Delhi so that the student photographers can implement the learned photographic skills in real life. The JPEG hub coordinators and members (student photographers) often involved in capturing the photographs of different events (technical, cultural, international conferences, sports, etc.) conducted throughout the year at JIIT.

OBJECTIVE

  1. Promoting photography as an art form: A photography hub can showcase the artistic side of photography and provide a platform for photographers to exhibit their work.
  2. Creating connections and networking opportunities: A photography hub can provide a central location for people to connect and network with other photography professionals, leading to collaborations and partnerships.
  3. Driving innovation: A photography hub can encourage innovation and experimentation in photography, pushing the boundaries of what is possible and driving the field forward.

EVENTS ORGANISED BY THE HUB

  1. 30-30: An event where you need to find and capture a particular location in the campus.
  2. Just Do It: A task is given to you, which you have to do and record it.
  3. Snap Story: It is required to form a story with a limited number of snaps.
